Monthly Sale Analysis

sales_by_month = data.groupby('Order Month')['Sales'].sum().reset_index()
fig = px.line(sales_by_month,
              x='Order Month',
              y='Sales',
              title='Monthly Sales Analysis')
fig.show()

Analysis: The line chart displays total sales for each month. Observe peaks and valleys in sales throughout the year. Identify the months with the highest and lowest sales figures. This could be due to seasonality, promotions, or other factors.

Sales Analysis by Category

sales_by_category = data.groupby('Category')['Sales'].sum().reset_index()
fig = px.pie(sales_by_category,
             values='Sales',
             names='Category',
             hole=0.5,
             color_discrete_sequence=px.colors.qualitative.Pastel)

fig.update_traces(textposition='inside', textinfo='percent+label')
fig.update_layout(title_text='Sales Analysis by Category', title_font=dict(size=24))
fig.show()

Analysis: The pie chart shows the proportion of total sales attributed to each product category. The largest slice represents the category with the highest sales, while smaller slices indicate lower sales for other categories. This visualization quickly reveals the dominant product categories and their relative contribution to overall revenue.

Sales and Profit Analysis by Segment

sales_profit_by_segment = data.groupby('Segment').agg({'Sales': 'sum', 'Profit': 'sum'}).reset_index()

color_palette = colors.qualitative.Pastel

fig = go.Figure()
fig = fig.add_trace(go.Bar(x=sales_profit_by_segment['Segment'],
                           y=sales_profit_by_segment['Sales'],
                           name='Sales',
                           marker_color=color_palette[0]))

fig.add_trace(go.Bar(x=sales_profit_by_segment['Segment'],
                     y=sales_profit_by_segment['Profit'],
                     name='Profit',
                     marker_color=color_palette[1]))

fig.update_layout(title_text='Sales and Profit Analysis by Segment',
                  xaxis_title='Customer Segment', yaxis_title='Amount')

fig.show()

Analysis: The "Sales and Profit Analysis by Segment" chart displays sales and profit figures for each customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office). The Consumer segment shows the highest sales and profit, indicating it's the most lucrative customer group. Comparing the sales and profit bars for each segment reveals the profitability of each segment. A segment with high sales but low profit may suggest pricing or cost issues requiring attention, whereas a segment with both high sales and high profit represents a successful and valuable customer group.
